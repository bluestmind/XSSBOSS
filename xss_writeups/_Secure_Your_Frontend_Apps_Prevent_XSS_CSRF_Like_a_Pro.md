Frontend security is often overlooked, but it’s critical for building reliable and professional web apps. This month, we’re focusing on practical ways to secure your frontend applications and avoid common pitfalls.

## 💡 Why Frontend Security Matters

Even if your backend is secure, your app can still be vulnerable to:

- XSS (Cross-Site Scripting): Malicious scripts injected into your app
- CSRF (Cross-Site Request Forgery): Unauthorized actions triggered by users
- Data leaks or compromised sessions

Ignoring these can cost you user trust and compromise your app’s integrity.

## 🛠 Key Security Best Practices

Here are some actionable steps you can take today:

- Sanitize Inputs: Always validate and escape user inputs before rendering.
- Use HTTP-Only Cookies: Protect sensitive data from JavaScript access.
- Enable Content Security Policy (CSP): Reduce the risk of XSS attacks.
- Anti-CSRF Tokens: Protect forms and API requests from unauthorized actions.
- Keep Dependencies Updated: Avoid vulnerabilities from outdated packages.

Small changes make a huge difference in protecting your users and projects.
